# Guide to Recording Test Results in Qatarina

This document explains in simple steps how testers can record results for test cases in Qatarina.  
It is designed for non‑technical users.

---

## Step 1: Sign In
- Open Qatarina.  
- Enter your username and password.  
- Once signed in, you will see your dashboard.  

---

## Step 2: Go to Your Inbox
- Click on **Inbox**.  
- Here you will find all test cases assigned to you.  
- Each test case shows the project name, so you know which project it belongs to.  

---

## Step 3: Open a Test Case
- Select the test case you want to work on.  
- A window will open with fields to fill in:  
  - **Observed Result** - What happened when you tested.  
  - **Notes** - Any comments about the testing process.  

---

## Step 4: Record the Outcome
- If the test worked as expected, click **Record Successful Test**.  
- If the test failed, click **Record Failed Test**.  
- Submitting creates a **Test Run** (this is just a record of your result).  

---

## Step 5: Review Your Test Results
- Each time you record a result, Qatarina saves it as a test run.  
- You can see your recorded results in the **Test Runs** section.  

---

## Step 6: Completing a Test Plan (for Leaders/Admins)
- Test plans are collections of test cases.  
- Leaders or admins can mark a test plan as **Complete** once all test runs are finished.  

---

## Key Points to Remember
- Always check the project name before recording results (to avoid confusion).  
- Fill in both fields: **Observed Result** and **Notes**.  
- Use the correct button: **Passed** or **Failed**.  
- Your role as a tester is mainly to record results — project setup, test plan creation, test run closing, and test plan completing/closing are handled by admins or leaders.
